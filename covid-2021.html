COVID-19 Writing Competition

Writing: May 2020

During Quincy's lockdown during the COVID-19 pandemic, Ian Cain, the Ward Three City Councilor, created the COVID-19 Writing Competition in Quincy, MA. There were over 800 entries to the competition and I am honored to be the recipient of the first place prize in the high school division.
