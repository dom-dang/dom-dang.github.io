Northeastern University Young Scholar's Program 2022

The summer entering my senior year of high school, I condcuted research in Dr. Amini's Lab about the mechanical properties of the tricuspid valve over time.

YSP

Science: June - August 2022

My research project for the summer was focused on the mechanical properties of the tricuspid valve - an important but understudied part of the heart. Using biaxial mechanical testing, my lab partner and I tested many anterior leaflet samples and graphed stress-strain plots to see if the modulus of stiffness changed over time.

What I learned:

  • Anatomy of the heart via porcine heart dissections
  • Lab technique: biaxial mechanical testing, PBS creation, suturing
  • Data analysis using MATLAB
