Will Mcdonough Writing Contest

Writing: 2020 - 2022

What started as an extra project in my English class in 9th grade, turned into 3 years of writing about my previous sports experiences. The Will McDonough Writing Contest is named in honor of the Boston Globe sportswriter. In 2020, over 1,100 students from across New England participated in the contest. I decided to write from my experience in marching band and during 2021 and the pandemic, I participated in the competition again and I wrote about my experience in a ballet dance class. I had the opportunity to win first place in the competition in 2020 and 2021. In 2022, I wrote about the summer when I learned how to surf and I received an honorable mention.
