Novartis Internship

Predicting Tumorigenic Potential of Human Cancer Cell Lines in Mice

Science | May 2025 – August 2025

Integrated bulk RNA-seq data with validated tumorigenicity outcomes to identify transcriptomic features linked to successful tumor formation, and trained a machine learning model to prioritize cell lines by their in vivo tumor-forming potential.

What I Learned:
  • Differential Gene Expression, Geneset Enrichment Analysis
  • Machine Learning (Random Forest, Naive Bayes, Logistic Regression, Linear Regression)
  • Scanpy Library with scRNA-seq data
Prizes Received:
  • 1st Place: Innovation in Research – Data Science
  • 2nd Place: Innovation in Research – Overall
  • 2nd Place: Innovation in Research – Scientific Content & Merit
  • 3rd Place: Poster Display & Presentation

Calo Lab

tRNA Analysis via Nanopore Sequencing

Science | Jan 2025 – Aug 2025

Analyzed Nanopore tRNA sequencing datasets to evaluate the method's ability to quantify tRNA abundance and detect tRNA fragments. Focused on samples with DDX1 perturbation to assess defects in tRNA maturation and fragment accumulation.

What I Learned:
  • Bioinformatics analysis of Nanopore sequencing data
  • NanoPlot (read QC), BWA (read alignment), DESeq2 (differential expression)

Bidirectional Promoters

Science | Jan 2024 – Jan 2025

Characterized bidirectional promoters (BDPs) as a tool for gene co-expression. Gained a deeper understanding of gene regulation with potential to measure BDP expression dynamics over time and apply BDPs in existing co-expression applications.

  • Plasmid construction: gel electrophoresis, PCR, Gibson assembly
  • Cell culture, FACS, fluorescence microscopy
  • MATLAB, Spotfire, FCSalyzer

Rouzbeh Amini Lab

Tricuspid Valve Mechanics

Science | June – August 2022

Researched the mechanical properties of the tricuspid valve in porcine hearts through the Young Scholars Program at Northeastern. Used biaxial mechanical testing and stress-strain plots to characterize changes in stiffness over time. Resulted in a publication in the Journal of Biomechanical Engineering (May 2023).

What I Learned:
  • Porcine heart dissection
  • Biaxial mechanical testing, sterile technique, suturing
  • MATLAB for biomechanics data analysis
